# Journey Blazers Marketing Analytics
### Systems Optimization Club — Project Member  

Journey Blazers is a Salesforce consulting firm with low brand awareness and limited advertising resources. Our team worked to design and analyze digital marketing strategies to help attract new clients.

## Overview
We researched advertising platforms, designed targeted LinkedIn ads, and developed an analytics approach to measure performance and cost efficiency for Journey Blazers’ campaigns.

## Tools & Skills
Python, Excel, SQL, Data Visualization, Marketing Analytics, Dashboard Planning

## My Contributions
- Helped research and select digital platforms (with a focus on LinkedIn) aligned with Journey Blazers’ B2B audience.
- Supported the design of ads targeting executives, Salesforce employees, and account executives to drive relevant leads.
- Worked on structuring metrics and dashboard ideas (e.g., cost per click, cost per lead, conversion rate) for use in Looker Studio once performance data became available.
- Participated in case study-style analytics exercises to practice using Python and SQL for trend and KPI analysis.
